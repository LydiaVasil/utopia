# የዩቶፕያ እሳቤዎች በመጽሓፍ ቅዱስ (Utopian Thoughts in the Bible)

An interdisciplinary course exploring utopian thought, history, and its connections to education, society, and civilization.

## What you’ll learn
- The origins and history of utopian writing and “blueprint” societies 
- Major works such as **Utopia** (1516) and **City of the Sun** (1603) 
- Utopianism, identity, and civilization—especially in the Ethiopian historical context 
- How utopian ideas relate to modern cultural, political, and educational challenges

## Course format
- **Consecutive lessons** (Lesson 1, Lesson 2, Lesson 3…)
- Each lesson includes a video and notes
- Optional quiz at the end of each lesson (coming soon)
